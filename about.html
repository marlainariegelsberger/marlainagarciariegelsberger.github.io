Marlaina Garcia Riegelsberger (she/they) is a queer performance artist based in Queens. A graduate of NYU Tisch Dance, Marlaina has had the opportunity to perform with the Scottish Ballet Youth Ensemble at The Joyce Theater, as well as in works by Yin Yue, Christina Robson, and Merce Cunningham. 

Marlaina has guest performed with dance companies such as Backhausdance, MotionTribe, and GoodTrouble Makers in Los Angeles, and abroad with Martz Contemporary Dance Company (Barcelona) and GAUCompany (Berlin). Other freelance work in New York City includes performances at Battery Dance Festival, ArtsOnSite, 14th Street Y with Brandon Woolf, The Tank NYC, Dance Gallery Festival (New York/Texas), CPR, The Issue Project Room, Green Space and the Baryshnikov Arts Center as a part of the Merce Cunningham Centennial. During the pandemic, Marlaina co-founded/hosted Harvest, a socially-distant seasonal performance party focusing on the witnessing and gathering of artists in the community.
